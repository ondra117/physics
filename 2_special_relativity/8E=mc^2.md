## dva paprsky na obě strany s energii $\frac{L}{2}$
$$
L_r = \frac{L}{2} \to pravo\\
L_l = \frac{L}{2} \to levo\\
E_0 = E_1 + L
$$
## ve vlaku co jede do prava $v$
$$
E^{\prime} = E\gamma\left(1 - \frac{v\cos(\varphi)}{c}\right)\\
L_r^{\prime} = L_r\gamma\left(1 - \frac{v\cos(0)}{c}\right) = L_r\gamma\left(1 - \frac{v}{c}\right)\\
L_l^{\prime} = L_l\gamma\left(1 - \frac{v\cos(\pi)}{c}\right) = L_l\gamma\left(1 + \frac{v}{c}\right)\\
L^{\prime} = L_r^{\prime} + L_l^{\prime} = L_r\gamma\left(1 - \frac{v}{c}\right) + L_l\gamma\left(1 + \frac{v}{c}\right) = \frac{L}{2}\gamma\left(\left(1 - \frac{v}{c}\right) + \left(1 + \frac{v}{c}\right)\right) = \frac{L}{2}\gamma\left(1 - \frac{v}{c} + 1 + \frac{v}{c}\right) = L\gamma\\
L^{\prime} = L\gamma
$$
$$
E_0 = E_1 + L\\
E_0^{\prime} = E_1^{\prime} + L^{\prime}\\
E_0^{\prime} - E_0 = E_1^{\prime} - E_1 + L^{\prime} - L\\
E^{\prime} - E = K\\
K_0 = K_1 + L^{\prime} - L\\
K_0 - K_1 = L^{\prime} - L\\
K_0 - K_1 = L\gamma - L\\
K_0 - K_1 = L(\gamma - 1)\\
K = (\gamma - 1)mc^2\\
(\gamma - 1)m_0c^2 - (\gamma - 1)m_1c^2 = L(\gamma - 1)\\
m_0c^2 - m_1c^2 = L\\
(m_0 - m_1)c^2 = L\\
\Delta mc^2 = L
$$
$$
E = mc^2

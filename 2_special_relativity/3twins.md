# Twins
$$
v = 0.8c\\
x = 4ly
$$
$$
t = \frac{x}{v} = 5\\
P = (t = 0, x = 0)\\
O = (t = 5, x = 4)\\
R = (t = 10, x = 0)\\
x_{P \to O} = \frac{x - vt}{\sqrt{\left(1- \frac{v^2}{c^2}\right)}} = \frac{4 - 0.8 \cdot 5}{\sqrt{\left(1- \frac{v^2}{c^2}\right)}} = 0\\
t_{P \to O} = \frac{t - \frac{vx}{c^2}}{\sqrt{\left(1- \frac{v^2}{c^2}\right)}} = \frac{5 - 0.8 \cdot 4}{\sqrt{\left(1- 0.8^2\right)}} = 3\\
R - O = (t = 5, x = -4)\\
x_{O \to R} = \frac{x - vt}{\sqrt{\left(1- \frac{v^2}{c^2}\right)}} = \frac{-4 + 0.8 \cdot 5}{\sqrt{\left(1- \frac{v^2}{c^2}\right)}} = 0\\
t_{O \to R} = \frac{t - \frac{vx}{c^2}}{\sqrt{\left(1- \frac{v^2}{c^2}\right)}} = \frac{5 - 0.8 \cdot 4}{\sqrt{\left(1- 0.8^2\right)}} = 3\\
$$
$$
x_{P \to R} = x_{P \to O} + x_{O \to R} = 0\\
t_{P \to R} = t_{P \to O} + t_{O \to R} = 6\\
t = 10
$$

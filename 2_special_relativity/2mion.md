# Mion
$$
t^{\prime} = 2.2us\\
v = 0.999c\\
l = 10000m
$$
# Bez relativity
$$
t=\frac{l}{v} = \frac{10000}{0.999c} = 33.3us
$$
# Relativita
## pohled ze země
$$
t = \frac{t^{\prime} + \frac{vx^{\prime}}{c^2}}{\sqrt{\left(1- \frac{v^2}{c^2}\right)}} = \frac{t^{\prime}}{\sqrt{\left(1- \frac{v^2}{c^2}\right)}} = 49.5us
$$
## pohled ze mionu
$$
l = \frac{l^{\prime} + vt^{\prime}}{\sqrt{\left(1- \frac{v^2}{c^2}\right)}}\\
l\sqrt{\left(1- \frac{v^2}{c^2}\right)} = l^{\prime}\\
l^{\prime} = 316.2m\\
t = \frac{l^{\prime}}{v} = 1.5us
$$

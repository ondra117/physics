# Co máme
## náboj -> náboj
$$
F = \frac{1}{4\pi\varepsilon_0}\frac{qQ}{r^2}
$$
## Uzvařený náboj z hustoty náboje
$$
Q = \int_V \rho\ dV
$$
# náboj -> pole -> náboj
$$
F = Eq = \frac{1}{4\pi\varepsilon_0}\frac{qQ}{r^2}\\
E = \frac{1}{4\pi\varepsilon_0}\frac{Q}{r^2}
$$
# Tok z krze kouli o R
$$
\oint_S E\ dA = \oint_S \frac{1}{4\pi\varepsilon_0}\frac{Q}{R^2}\ dA = \frac{1}{4\pi\varepsilon_0}\frac{Q}{R^2} \cdot 4\pi R^2 = \frac{Q}{\varepsilon_0}
$$
# E div
$$
\int_V \nabla\cdot E\ dV = \oint_S E\ dA = \frac{Q}{\varepsilon_0} = \frac{\int_V \rho\ dV}{\varepsilon_0} = \int_V \frac{\rho}{\varepsilon_0}\ dV\\
\int_V \nabla\cdot E\ dV = \int_V \frac{\rho}{\varepsilon_0}\ dV
$$
$$
\nabla\cdot E = \frac{\rho}{\varepsilon_0}
$$
